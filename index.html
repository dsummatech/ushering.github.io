OUR SERVICES

HOSTESS

Want to give your guests and clients a VIP and professional impression? Our Hostesses are not only beautiful, but friendly, professional, proactive and attentive to details. They support both the hospitality and the logistics of your event, welcoming guests and ensuring they’re well catered for.

PROMOTIONAL STAFF

Want to attract new customers and push sales for your products? Using enthusiastic and persuasive skills, our Promotional Staff will confidently approach and engage with new customers, promote your products to create awareness and make sales at busy shopping malls and tradeshows.

CONFERENCE ASSISTANTS

We know the importance of projecting a positive and professional corporate image of your brand to delegates and speakers. Our experienced and professional Conference Assistants would manage the efficient running of registration, badge scanning, etc. at your conference.

EXHIBITION STAFF

Looking to generate new leads at exhibitions or tradeshows? Our intelligent Exhibition Staff would interact with show visitors, distribute samples and marketing material and capture data of prospects. With English and French speaking staff, they orient prospects at your booth on your products and offers.
